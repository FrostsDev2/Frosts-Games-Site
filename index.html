RIP Frosts Games
7-8-2025 – 3-5-2026

Hello everyone. I'm Frosts, the creator of Frosts Games. After a lot of thought, I’ve decided that I will be shutting down the site.

Frosts Games originally started as a small project where students could relax, play simple games, and spend time together online. The goal was never anything serious — just a place for people to take a break and enjoy themselves with friends.

Over time, however, the environment around school networks has become more restrictive. Many schools continue to block harmless websites and games, even ones that contain no violence and no harmful content. In many ways this begins to feel like a form of digital censorship, where students lose access to simple ways of relaxing or connecting with others.

Because of the attention the site has received and the complications that can come with it, I’ve decided that for my own protection and peace of mind, the best decision is to close the site.

The Minecraft server Frosts SMP will also be shutting down along with the website.

I still believe games can help people bond, make friends, and build communities — especially for students who spend most of their day at school. Frosts Games was built around that idea, and I’m grateful for everyone who played on the site while it lasted.

Thank you to everyone who supported the project.

— Frosts
