Frosts Games
A small project that meant a lot
RIP Frosts Games July 8, 2025 — March 5, 2026

Hello everyone. I'm Frosts, the person who created Frosts Games.

This site started as a small idea — just a place where people could open a tab, play a simple game, and relax for a few minutes. Over time it became something I spent a huge amount of my free time building and maintaining.

Late nights fixing things. Updating games. Trying to keep the site running. Making sure friends and other students had somewhere they could go to take a break and enjoy themselves.

The games here were always simple and non-violent. The goal was never to cause problems — it was just to give students a place to unwind and connect with each other online.

Unfortunately, the reality is that many schools are becoming increasingly restrictive with websites and online games. Even harmless projects like this often end up being blocked or treated as something that shouldn't exist on school networks. To me, that starts to feel a lot like a form of digital censorship against simple entertainment.

Because of the attention the site has received and the potential trouble it could cause, I’ve decided that I need to take the site down for my own protection.

The Minecraft server Frosts SMP will also be shutting down.

It’s honestly difficult to close something that I put so much time and effort into. What started as a small project slowly turned into something I really cared about, and seeing it end like this is hard.

Still, I'm grateful for everyone who visited the site, played the games, or spent time on the server. Even if this project didn’t last forever, the time people spent enjoying it was real.

Thank you to everyone who was part of Frosts Games while it existed.

— Frosts
